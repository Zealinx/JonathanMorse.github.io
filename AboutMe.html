About Me

Hello! I'm Jonathan Morse and I have always had a fascination and passion for programming and video games. The people who could make a computer do what they want always seemed like wizards to me and to be able to do that myself now is crazy for me to think about. Along the path of my education I have acquired skills in C, C++, and C#, and have learned to use tools such as Unity and the OpenGL API. One of the most important things I have learned is the ability to work on a cross-discipline team for larger projects. Though it is a difficult task, to be able to pull together all of these different experiences to create a single product is amazing to me. As my graduation from DigiPen has come and gone, I'm looking for the next step in my journey into this industry that I love.
